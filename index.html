Research Projects

  1. Expository
    A random matrix approach to the existence of tracial states on unital stably finite exact C*-algebras
    2023
    Abs PDF

    We give a self contained and concise overview of a random matrix approach to showing the existence of tracial states on unital exact stably finite C*-algebras.

  2. Expository
    A∞-absolute continuity of elliptic measures in 1-sided chord-arc domains
    2023
    Abs PDF

    We detail a recent result on a Carleson measure condition for elliptic measures in 1-sided chord arc domains to belong to the A∞ class of Muckenhoupt weights.

  3. Thesis
    On the strong continuity and local equicontinuity of Ornstein-Uhlenbeck semigroups in open sets of Hilbert spaces
    2022
    Abs

    This project gives an exposition of the C0-property of Ornstein-Uhlenbeck semigroups in the space of bounded continuous functions on a separable Hilbert space H with respect to the mixed topology, as well as a partial extension of this result to the case of the restricted semigroup to an open subset of H.
